Creativity

Creativity would be my greatest strength. I love art, I love colours and I am intrigued by the different aspects of art and artists. Open Minded

I am usually conservative in how I dress, in constrast to how I viewed situations. Having a liberal mind is my greatest strength as my ability to think through and analyze situations is always without prejudice. I am open to constructive criticism and suggestions. I treat each individual who crosses my path with respect and accept their uniqueness as every individual is different. This attribute has helped me to appreciate that everyone's mind and body is different and as such, appreciates the diverse world I live in. Subsequently, I am motivated to experiment with different hair colours which should influence my conservative wardrobe to move towards one that accentuates my liberal mind.

PERSONALITY TOOLS




Similar Minds

After using the Similar Minds Personality Tool, I realised that there were no careers in the computer field listed.I was a bit dissappointed, but it also occurred to me that while the obvious was not listed, my degree could be interwoven in some of the career choices on the list. For example, a data analyst may need a computer programmer to write a programme that will analyze data efficiently so that a Marketing Manager (on the list) may be better able to market his/her company's products. Therefore, this tool is effective, as it gave me a variety of career choices, that would seemed to be outside of my periphery, but has given me optimism. Finally, I am not deterred by the dictator career choice either, as this in my mind only confirms how strong minded I am.
